# üåü Day of AI ‚Äî Overview & Curriculum Breakdown

The **Day of AI** is a global **free educational initiative** created by **MIT RAISE** (Responsible AI for Social Empowerment and Education), a collaboration between the **MIT Media Lab** and the **MIT Schwarzman College of Computing**. Its mission is to equip **K‚Äì12 students and teachers** with foundational knowledge of artificial intelligence, emphasizing ethical use, hands-on exploration, and digital citizenship.

Day of AI provides **ready-to-use curriculum**, **teacher guides**, and **age-appropriate activities** that schools can run on *any day they choose*. All materials are **completely free**.
